# 🎙️ Production Framework
**Cartoon Quantum Mechanics — RossFive & Aurphyx**

---

## Audio Architecture

### Layer 1: Marvin Narration (Character Voice)
- **Source:** Ross's raw voice
- **Treatment:** Character filter — dry, slightly elevated pitch, precise cadence, cosmically offended affect
- **Mic setup:** Clean dry recording, apply filter in post
- **Tone guidance:** Marvin is never surprised. Marvin is always mildly disappointed in the universe but professionally committed to narrating its failures.

### Layer 2: Formal Definitions (Raw Voice)
- **Source:** Ross's raw voice, NO filter
- **Treatment:** None — professorial authority, measured pace
- **Mic setup:** Same clean dry recording
- **Tone guidance:** This is the "real" voice of the channel. Authoritative. Precise. Grounded. The contrast with Marvin is intentional and jarring in a good way.

### Layer 3: Aurphyx SUXS Segment
- **Source:** Ross's raw voice
- **Treatment:** Slightly more conversational, self-aware, fourth-wall adjacent
- **Tone guidance:** The voice of someone who has been doing serious research and is also very aware that Wile E. Coyote has been solving his engineering problems since 1949.

### Layer 4: Expert Clips (≤28 seconds each)
- **Sources:** NDT (Neil deGrasse Tyson), Richard Feynman archival, Roger Penrose, Sean Carroll, Anton Zeilinger
- **Acquisition methods:**
  - YouTube Remix feature (attribution-compliant, educational use)
  - Podcast audio chops (transformative use, clearly labeled)
  - Archival lecture footage (check individual copyright status)
- **On-screen treatment:** Lower-third label: **"PROFESSOR [NAME] ON [CONCEPT]"** — maintains academic framing
- **Marvin response:** Every expert clip is immediately followed by a Marvin one-liner that comments on it. The expert gets 28 seconds. Marvin gets 4 seconds. The 4 seconds win.

## Distribution Framework

### RossFive Channel
- **Content:** Full episodes as produced
- **Aurphyx SUXS:** 2–3 minute version (teaser cut)
- **Titles (SEO format):** "[Quantum Concept] Explained by Wile E. Coyote" or "Why [Cartoon Moment] Is Actually Quantum Physics"
- **Description template:** Educational framing, timestamps, links to Aurphyx channel for extended research segment
- **Tags:** quantum mechanics, quantum physics, looney tunes, physics education, wave function, [concept-specific]

### Aurphyx Channel
- **Content:** Same episodes + extended Aurphyx SUXS segments (5–8 minutes)
- **Extended segment content:** Deeper research dive, technical Aurphyx architecture detail, research paper references
- **Titles:** More research-forward — "[Concept] and the [Aurphyx Architecture Problem]"
- **Audience:** Quantum computing, AI/ML research, theoretical physics communities

### Cross-Promotion
- RossFive episodes end with: "The full Aurphyx research breakdown is on the Aurphyx channel — link in description"
- Aurphyx episodes reference: "The cartoon explainer for this concept is on RossFive"
- Shared clips create YouTube algorithmic cross-pollination

## The Non-Negotiables (Anti-Basic Bitch Protocol)

1. **No concept gets explained once.** It gets hit from the cartoon angle, the physics angle, and the Aurphyx angle — all within the same episode.
2. **Every episode has a duality thread.** Plant → Return → Detonation. If it's not in the script, it's not done.
3. **Marvin always gets the last word on expert clips.** 28 seconds of expert, 4 seconds of Marvin. The 4 seconds win.
4. **The Aurphyx SUXS segment is never optional.** It is the reason the channel exists. The cartoon and the physics are the delivery mechanism.
5. **Wile E. holds a sign.** Every episode. Non-negotiable. It's his tenure requirement.
