## Core Concept

The Observer Effect: the act of measurement disturbs the system being measured. In quantum mechanics, this goes deeper than classical disturbance — the observer is not separate from the experiment. The observer is part of the experiment. Every time Wile E. looks at the camera, the physics changes. This is not a cartoon gag. This is the most accurate depiction of quantum measurement in the history of television.
