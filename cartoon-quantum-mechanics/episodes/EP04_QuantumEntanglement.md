## Core Concept

Quantum entanglement: two particles become correlated such that the state of one instantly determines the state of the other, regardless of distance. Einstein called it "spooky action at a distance" and hated it. The Road Runner and Wile E. Coyote are entangled. Their quantum states are correlated across the entire desert: whenever Road Runner is free, Wile E. is falling. Always. Instantly. Regardless of distance. Bell's theorem proved Einstein wrong. Road Runner proved it funnier.

---

## Duality Thread: "They've Never Touched And Yet One Always Falls"

- **Plant:** Road Runner goes left. Coyote falls right. Nobody touched anybody.
- **Return:** Bell's theorem — the correlations are too perfect to be coincidence, too instantaneous to be communication
- **Detonation:** "Entanglement is not a connection between two things. It is the proof that they were never fully separate to begin with. Wile E. and Road Runner are not predator and prey. They are a single quantum system. The falling is not caused by the running. They are the same event, expressed in two particles."
