## Core Concept

Quantum superposition: a system exists in multiple states simultaneously until measured. Schrödinger's Cat is the classic thought experiment — but Wile E. Coyote *is* Schrödinger's Cat. Every time an ACME package arrives, it simultaneously contains the solution to all his problems AND the cause of his next catastrophic failure. Until he opens it.
