## **XXXV.3 Gradient Dynamics**

Gradient Dynamics occur when:

- HRD is low  
- VIM is moderate  
- curvature is smooth  
- imbalance is small  

The system follows:

\[
\frac{d\beta}{dt} \approx -2(\beta - 1)\nabla^2 \beta
\]

### Behaviors
- monotonic convergence  
- smooth flow lines  
- stable geodesics  
- predictable evolution  

Gradient Dynamics dominate **near Equilibrium Manifold**.
