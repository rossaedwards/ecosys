- **Conjunction**  
  \[
  \beta_1 \wedge \beta_2 = \min(\beta_1,\ \beta_2)

  \[

- **Implication**  
  \[
  \beta_1 \Rightarrow \beta_2 = \max(1 - \beta_1,\ \beta_2)

  Imbalance implies correction.

- **Equivalence**  
  \[
  \beta_1 \Leftrightarrow \beta_2 = 1 - |\beta_1 - \beta_2|

These operators form the **Balance Logical Algebra**.
