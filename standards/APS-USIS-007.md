The Global Voting System (GVS) is the decentralized, identity‑anchored, continuity‑verified, ethically‑bounded voting and consensus engine of the Universal Soul Identity System (USIS).

GVS ensures:

One Soul → One Identity → One Vote

transparent, tamper‑proof governance

off‑grid voting capability

identity‑anchored legitimacy

SAGES‑aligned ethics

AuraFS‑anchored provenance

SoulSync‑verified presence

SoulKey‑verified continuity

BlissID‑verified sovereignty

GVS is the governance execution layer of the Aurphyx Universe Stack.

8. Ballot Lifecycle
8.1 Generation
GVS generates a ballot template based on the governance event.

8.2 Verification
SoulSync verifies presence.
SoulKey verifies continuity.
BlissID verifies sovereignty.

8.3 Casting
The voter signs the ballot locally.

8.4 Consensus
Archivus validates the ballot and reaches finality.

8.5 Ledger Recording
The Ineffable Ledger stores the ballot immutably.
