A decentralized, identity‑anchored, provenance‑preserving ledger that records all governance, identity, and economic events.

The consensus mechanism ensuring ethical, identity‑coherent, reversible, and transparent ledger finality.

A block containing identity, governance, or economic events with GuardHash lineage.
