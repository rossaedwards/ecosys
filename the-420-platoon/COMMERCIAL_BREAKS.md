# 📺 The 420 Platoon — Commercial Break Formats

---

## BREAK 1 — The Claymation Historical Fight (2 min)

**Format:** Celebrity Deathmatch × Drunk History × WWE entrance cards

**Structure:**
```
[DR. SATAN INTRO — 15 sec]
  → Crypt Keeper energy, sets up the combatants
  → Always opens with: "WELCOME, you magnificent disasters..."

[FIGHTER INTROS — 20 sec each]
  → Wrestling-style entrance cards
  → Absurd historical footnotes as stats
  → Example: "Napoleon | 5'7" | Conquered Europe | Lost to a bunny"

[THE FIGHT — 60 sec]
  → Pure claymation chaos
  → Weapons are historically adjacent but absurd
  → Physics are wrong in consistent, funny ways
  → At least one completely anachronistic element

[DR. SATAN OUTRO — 15 sec]
  → "And THAT, my fetid little friends..."
  → Delivers the actual historical lesson
  → Lesson is always grimly relevant to the episode's theme
```

## BREAK 2 — Avatar × Claymation Dialogue (2 min)

**Format:** Tropic Thunder meta-commitment × PBS documentary × actual facts

**Structure:**
```
[SETTING — 10 sec]
  → Simple environment: tavern, bunker, park bench, etc.
  → Realistic avatar character from current episode
  → Claymation historical figure waiting

[DIALOGUE — 90 sec]
  → Historical figure has accurate knowledge BUT stoner interpretation
  → Avatar character represents current-day perspective
  → Conversation is about the episode's events through historical lens
  → Always contains 2-3 actual historical facts
  → Always contains 1 completely fabricated "fact" that sounds real

[BUTTON — 20 sec]
  → Historical figure gives accidentally correct advice
  → Avatar leaves. Historical figure addresses camera.
  → Historical figure says something that recontextualizes the episode
```
