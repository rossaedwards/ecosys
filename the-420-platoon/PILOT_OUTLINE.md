## COLD OPEN — "The Drop (Origin)"

**Scene:** A bombed-out corporate megacity skyline, 2045. Drone warfare overhead. The 420 Platoon is sitting in a crater, passing a blunt, completely unbothered.

- **Chunky (new):** "Shouldn't we be... doing something?"
- **Blaze:** *[exhales]* "We ARE doing something. We're maintaining morale."
- Pop-Up Video bubble: *🎵 WHOOP WHOOP 🎵 "The U.S. Military spends $180 per gallon on jet fuel. A gram costs $8. Do the math."*
- Enemy faction advances. The Platoon notices. Begins roasting them mid-advance.
- **Roxy:** "Oh look. The CorpSec boys. Did you coordinate your matching khakis or is that a subscription service?"
- Enemy soldiers visibly falter.
- Entire Platoon drops joints simultaneously.
- Platoon bends to retrieve. Enemy soldiers open fire — hit each other's weapons, trip on drones, detonate their own smoke grenades.
- Platoon retrieves joints. Surveys carnage.
- **DRILL SERGEANT MATERIALIZES:** *"EVEN THESE PUSSIES KNOW THE WAR ISN'T WORTH IT, WAKE THE FUCK UP YOU PANSIES!!"*
- Drill Sergeant vanishes.
- **Blaze:** *"...Yeah. This is fine."*
- TITLE CARD: **THE 420 PLATOON** *(vinyl scratch sound)*

## COMMERCIAL BREAK 2 — "Blaze Meets Benjamin Franklin"

**AVATAR × CLAYMATION DIALOGUE**

- Blaze (realistic avatar) sits across from claymation Ben Franklin at a tavern table
- Franklin has a blunt. Obviously.
- **Franklin:** "You know I invented bifocals because I couldn't read the constitution I was too high to write."

- Blaze returns to battle with Franklin's colonial stoner wisdom
