## 🔥 Elevator Pitch

A heroic stoner army navigates apocalyptic dystopian chaos with complete laid-back detachment. Modern-day MASH for Gen X and Millennials — dead serious situations, absurd contrasting humor, meta movie references, and a vinyl deck VJ remixing archival history as the narrative engine.

**Living characters** = realistic avatars.  
**Dead historical figures** = claymation.  
**The war always loses.** The joint always wins.
