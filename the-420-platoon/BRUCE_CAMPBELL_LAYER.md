## Bruce Campbell as Tonal North Star

Ash Williams is the show's **spirit animal** because:
1. He is perpetually in over his head
2. He refuses to acknowledge this
3. He wins anyway through sheer absurdist confidence
4. He makes one-liners in the face of cosmic horror
5. This is EXACTLY what the 420 Platoon does

Blaze Hawkins IS Ash Williams if Ash Williams discovered cannabis instead of the Necronomicon. Same energy. Different weapon.
