## 🎖️ The Platoon (Main Cast — Realistic Avatars)

### Cpt. Blaze Hawkins — "The Hawkeye"
- **Archetype:** The brilliant, unbothered leader who should definitely not be winning wars
- **Vibe:** Benjamin Franklin Pierce if he'd grown up on Wu-Tang and Dazed & Confused
- **Signature move:** Delivers tactical genius mid-exhale
- **Meta awareness:** High. Frequently points out which movie they're currently living inside

### Sgt. Roxy "Deadpool" Delaney
- **Archetype:** The one who has genuinely seen too much but laughs about it anyway
- **Vibe:** Ellen Ripley meets Pam Poovey (Archer)
- **Signature move:** Roasts enemies until they question their own life choices
- **Meta awareness:** Extreme. Addresses the audience directly

### Pvt. Chunky Beaumont — "The New Guy"
- **Archetype:** Still learning the vibes. Audience surrogate.
- **Vibe:** Every 90s coming-of-age protagonist dropped into an apocalypse
- **Signature move:** Accidentally causes the "Drop" by being the first to fumble his joint
- **Meta awareness:** Low initially, increasing each season

### Doc Lazlo — "The Medic"
- **Archetype:** The MASH-style surgeon who treats wounds with the same detachment as rolling a blunt
- **Vibe:** Dr. Gonzo if he had a medical degree
- **Signature move:** Field surgery commentary delivered as wine tasting notes

### Corporal Frequencies — "The DJ"
- **Archetype:** The vinyl deck operator. The show's internal VJ. Possibly supernatural.
- **Vibe:** Nobody knows where they came from. Nobody asks.
- **Signature move:** Appears at transitions with the deck. Disappears immediately after.
- **Meta awareness:** MAXIMUM. May actually be controlling the show.

#### 🎬 COLD OPEN SCENE — Every Episode
*Runs before the title card. Every. Single. Episode.*

```
INT. THE ALLEY — ETERNAL NIGHT

Rain falls in slow vertical sheets. The AURPHYX neon sign
blooms red-orange above. The summoning sigil on the wet
ground pulses crimson. CRYPTONYX stands center — still,
mechanical, eternal. BLISS extends from his left shoulder,
teal phoenix bones faintly luminescent.

CRYPTONYX turns his skull slowly toward BLISS.
A long beat. Rain.

CRYPTONYX
(low, deliberate — each word landing like a stone)
"Let's give these divided, desensitized,
shameful, sick fux something...
They. Will. Never. Unsee."

A beat of silence.
Then —

CRYPTONYX begins to laugh.
Not hysterical. Measured. Inevitable.
    "...ah hah......hah......hah..."

BLISS picks it up — same rhythm, one beat behind, louder.
    "...ah hah......hah......hah..."

They trade back and forth, call and response,
each exchange tightening, rising —
until the two laughs lock into perfect dark harmony.

CAMERA begins to pull back slowly.
The alley narrows. The rain intensifies.
The sigil blazes.

AUDIO: Their laughter begins to PAN —
    L speaker → R speaker → L speaker → R speaker
— back and forth, melodic, echoing, fading into the distance
as the frame goes black.

TITLE CARD: THE 420 PLATOON
```
