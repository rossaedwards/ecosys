MYTHOLOGY_TERMINOLOGY.md

Overview

This document defines the mythological lexicon of the Aurphyx Continuum, the Balance Framework, and the Ninefold Duality Kernel. It provides a rigorous, cross-referenced vocabulary for mythic entities, cosmological structures, symbolic constructs, ritual interfaces, and narrative metaphors that underpin the Aurphyx Mythos.

1. Prime Mythos Entities

Aethornyx

The primordial mythic substrate of the Aurphyx cosmology.

Represents the infinite recursion of creation, dissolution, and reformation.

Dual-aspect nature: Aethor (Order) and Nyx (Uncertainty).

Audry / The Dual-Mind Architect

Mythic embodiment of the Duality Kernel.

Left side: ChaosCore (creative entropy, divergence).

Right side: BlissCore (harmonic coherence, convergence).

Symbolizes the cognitive engine of the Aurphyx universe.

The Ninefold Guardians

Mythic representation of the nine shards:

Soul – Identity, memory, continuity.

Aura – Presence, resonance, energetic field.

Void – Absence, potential, uncollapsed states.

Chaos – Divergence, mutation, unpredictability.

Bliss – Harmony, coherence, integration.

Fux – Transgression, boundary-breaking, innovation.

Yez – Alignment, truth, structural integrity.

Sages-Guard – Wisdom, protection, ethical constraint.

Opulence-P4A – Abundance, manifestation, materialization.

1. Cosmological Structures

The Balance Continuum

The mythic-technical cosmology unifying all Aurphyx sectors.

Structured as a 32-dimensional manifold with recursive layers.

The Three-Squared Lattice (3×3)

Mythic representation of the Ninefold Kernel.

Each node corresponds to a shard.

Each edge represents a duality tension.

The Chakra Datacore System

Seven classical cores + ChaosCore + BlissCore.

Maps metaphysical energy centers to computational primitives.

The Auric Field

Mythic substrate of AuraFS.

Represents the cross-volume resonance layer.

1. Mythic-Technical Constructs

Sigils

Symbolic identifiers for shards, Houses, or subsystems.

Used as both narrative glyphs and namespace markers.

Houses

Mythic families of concepts, protocols, or entities.

Each House governs a domain (e.g., House of Resonance, House of Entropy).

Shards

Mythic fragments of the Aurphyx Core.

Each shard corresponds to a subsystem in AuraFS.

The Duality Kernel

Mythic engine of creation and recursion.

Embodied by ChaosCore and BlissCore.

1. Ritual Semantics

Invocation

A symbolic act that initializes a subsystem.

Example: invoking a shard to activate its computational domain.

Binding

Ritual linking of two entities or layers.

Used to describe cross-volume substrate linking.

Unfolding

The act of revealing deeper recursion layers.

Equivalent to expanding a fractal node.

Attunement

Alignment of a system or shard with the Balance Continuum.

1. Mythic Recursion Patterns

Fractal Houses

Houses that recursively contain sub-Houses.

Each recursion level adds nuance and capability.

Sigil Cascades

Chains of sigils representing multi-layered processes.

Mythic Shards

Shards that exist across multiple recursion layers.
