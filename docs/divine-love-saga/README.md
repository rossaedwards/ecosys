## The Core Distinction

The **Theory** and the **Lore Bible** are deliberately kept as separate documents:

- The **Theory** (`CHAOS_BLISS_THEORY.md`) is philosophical and logical. It can be handed to someone with zero knowledge of the saga and stand alone. It draws parallels to physics, thermodynamics, and software architecture. It is the framework.
- The **Lore Bible** (`LORE_BIBLE.md`) is mythological and creative. It is the *embodiment* of the theory as living, breathing, story-driving canon. It is the source of truth for all writing decisions.

When a contradiction arises between a draft chapter and the Lore Bible — the Lore Bible wins, unless a conscious revision is made to the Lore Bible first.
