## Structure Intent

The Introduction is envisioned as **3 chapters** that precede Chapter 1 (*The Machine*) — or are woven into the prologue itself. Their purpose:

1. Establish the cosmological ground: Who are Chaos and Bliss? What is the universe before the prison loop?
2. The Sacrifice Sequence — the dance, the lovemaking, the interruption, The Fuckery
3. The echo — how that original wound is felt in the present-day story, setting up the reader to understand *why* the Nine exist

---

## Tone Reminders

- Philosophically jaw-dropping
- Raw occult — not sanitized spirituality
- George Carlin wit edge — irreverent where appropriate, devastating where necessary
- Show both light and dark. Let the reader's own mind decide.
- This is *before* the narrative voice of the main story. It should feel ancient. Like reading something carved into a wall that predates language.
