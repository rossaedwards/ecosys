## Executive Summary

The Vibe Audio Protocol (V.A.P.) is a **9-dimensional metadata standard** designed to capture the **holographic identity** of an audio file. Unlike ID3 tags which capture *bibliographic* data (Who/What), V.A.P. captures *experiential* data (How/Why). It serves as the audio sensory codec for the **Symbiotic Integration Channel (SIC)** within the Aurphyx Universe Stack, translating raw DSP physics into the dimensional language of the TSLCA.

Metadata generation is orchestrated through three sequential phases:
- **Phase I (DSP):** Physical extraction
- **Phase II (ML/NLP):** Psychological inference
- **Phase III (I/O):** Environmental & social formatting

## Pillar 1: STRUCTURAL (The Skeleton)

*Governs the mathematical, temporal, and dynamic architecture.*

### 1.1 Temporal Dynamics

- **BPM (Raw):** Technical pulses per minute.
- **BPM (Perceived):** Half-time (0.5×), Double-time (2.0×), or Triplet-feel index.
- **Groove Quantization:**
  - *Machine Lock:* 100% rigid grid alignment (Techno/Trance)
  - *Human Swing:* Percentage of deviation (e.g., J Dilla Swing ~60%)
- **Time Signature:** Standard (4/4, 3/4) vs. Complex (7/8, 5/4)

### 1.2 Arrangement Architecture

- **Sectional Markers:** Timestamps for Intro, Verse, Chorus, Drop, Bridge, Outro.
- **Mix-Window Index:** Duration (bars) available for seamless DJ transitions.
- **Breakdown Depth:** Energy drop percentage during the bridge/breakdown.

### 1.3 Percussive DNA

- **Kick Transient Profile:**
  - *Attack:* Sharp (Click) vs. Soft (Thud)
  - *Decay:* Short (Tight) vs. Long (Boom/808)
- **Syncopation Index:** Ratio of on-beat to off-beat emphasis (0.0 – 1.0)
- **Ghost Note Density:** Frequency of low-velocity rhythmic subdivisions.

## Pillar 2: TONAL (The Flesh)

*Governs the harmonic, melodic, and pitch-based content.*

### 2.1 Harmonic Profile

- **Key Signature:** Root Note + Scale (Major, Minor, Dorian, Lydian, etc.)
- **Chord Complexity:**
  - *Triadic:* Simple Root-3rd-5th (Pop/Rock)
  - *Extended:* 7ths, 9ths, 11ths, 13ths (Jazz/Neo-Soul)
- **Dissonance Rating:** Frequency of tension intervals (Minor 2nds, Tritones)

### 2.2 Melodic Contour

- **Range Span:** Octave spread between lowest and highest melodic note.
- **Hook Strength:** Repetition frequency of the primary motif (Earworm Score).
- **Melodic Motion:** Conjunct (Step-wise) vs. Disjunct (Large leaps)

### 2.3 Tuning Standard

- **Reference Pitch:** 440Hz (Standard), 432Hz (Cosmic), 444Hz (Bright)
- **Microtonality:** Presence of non-western pitch bends or quarter tones.

## Pillar 3: TIMBRAL (The Skin)

*Governs the texture, fidelity, and sonic physics.*

### 3.1 Spectral Physics

- **Frequency Balance:**
  - *Sub-Dominant:* Energy < 60Hz
  - *Mid-Forward:* Energy 500Hz – 2kHz (Vocal/Guitar focus)
  - *Air/Brilliance:* Energy > 10kHz
- **Spectral Saturation:** Density of harmonics (Clean Sine vs. Distorted Square)

### 3.2 Production Aesthetic

- **Fidelity Score:**
  - *Lo-Fi:* Bit-crushing, limited bandwidth, noise floor
  - *Hi-Fi:* Full spectrum, pristine transient response
- **Dynamic Range (LRA):** Loudness Range Average (Audiophile score)
- **Spatial Width:** Mono, Stereo, or Immersive (Binaural/Atmos)

### 3.3 Texture Grain

- **Surface:** Glassy, Gritty, Wooden, Metallic, Liquid
- **Artifacts:** Vinyl crackle, Tape hiss, 60-cycle hum (intentional)

## Pillar 4: LINGUISTIC (The Voice)

*Governs the semantic message and vocal delivery.*

### 4.1 Semantic Content

- **Explicit Filter:** Clean, Mild, Explicit (Profanity), Severe (Hate/Violence)
- **Topic Clusters:** Love, Rebellion, Money, Depression, Party, Politics
- **Narrative Arc:** Linear Story vs. Abstract/Repetitive Mantra

### 4.2 Vocal Texture

- **Position:** Intimate (Close-mic), Distant (Reverb-soaked), Buried
- **Delivery Style:** Sung, Rapped, Screamed, Spoken, Whispered
- **Processing:** Dry, Auto-Tuned, Chopped & Screwed, Vocoded

### 4.3 Language Profile

- **Primary Language:** ISO 639-1 Code (en, es, fr, etc.)
- **Dialect/Slang:** Specific regional markers (e.g., Memphis Phonk, UK Drill)

## Pillar 5: AFFECTIVE (The Heart)

*Governs the psychological and emotional impact.*

### 5.1 The Thayer Coordinates

- **Valence:** Positivity (−1.0 to +1.0)
- **Arousal:** Energy Level (0.0 to 1.0)
- **Dominance:** Empowering/Aggressive vs. Submissive/Vulnerable

### 5.2 Emotional Complexity

- **Mood Stability:** Constant state vs. Volatile shifts
- **Catharsis Potential:** Suitability for emotional venting/release
- **Nostalgia Trigger:** Probability of evoking past memories (Era-dependent)

### 5.3 Tension Arc

- **Build-up Velocity:** Rate at which tension increases.
- **Resolution State:** Triumphant, Melancholic, or Unresolved (Cliffhanger)

*Governs environmental and situational tagging.*

### 6.1 Scenario Engine

- **Macro-Setting:** Gym, Car, Bedroom, Club, Nature, Office
- **Micro-Activity:** Night Drive, Heavy Lifting, Deep Work, Intimacy, Commute
- **Social Setting:** Solo, Couple, Small Group, Crowd/Mass

## Pillar 7: PHOTOMETRIC (The Eye)

*Governs visual data for lighting and AuraOS UI synchronization.*

### 7.1 Chromatic Map

- **Primary Hex:** Dominant color code (e.g., `#191970`). Format: `^#[0-9A-Fa-f]{6}$`
- **Secondary Hex:** Accent color code.
- **Palette Temperature:** Cool (Blues/Purples) vs. Warm (Reds/Oranges)

### 7.2 Lumen Dynamics

- **Brightness Floor/Ceiling:** Minimum vs. Maximum intensity (0.0 – 1.0)
- **Strobe Trigger:** Threshold for rapid light actuation.
- **Fade Rate:** Sharp (On/Off) vs. Smooth (Linear Fade)

### 7.3 Visual Texture

- **Fog Density:** Recommended haze level (0.0 – 1.0)
- **Laser Compatibility:** Suitability for beam effects.
- **Visual Noise:** Static/Glitch vs. Clean/Solid

## Pillar 8: KINETIC (The Body)

*Governs biometric and physical response data.*

### 8.1 Biometric Entrainment

- **Target HR Zone:** BPM range for heart rate lock (e.g., 110–130)
- **HRV Impact:** Stress induction (Low HRV) vs. Flow induction (High HRV)
- **Breath Rate:** Recommended breathing cadence.

### 8.2 Motor Response

- **Drive:** Impulse to move forward (Run/Walk)
- **Sway:** Impulse to rock side-to-side
- **Head-Nod:** Neck muscle activation index

### 8.3 Energy Expenditure

- **MET Score:** Metabolic Equivalent of Task — intensity of activity supported.

## Pillar 9: GENEALOGICAL (The Roots)

*Governs historical lineage, sampling, and cultural tribe.*

### 9.1 Era Anchoring

- **Release Date:** Official timestamp.
- **Cultural Era:** Perceived time (e.g., "Y2K," "Golden Era," "Baroque")
- **Timelessness Score:** How dated the production sounds (0.0 – 1.0)

### 9.2 DNA & Sampling

- **Sample Lineage:** Original sources used (e.g., "Amen Break")
- **Interpolation:** Melodies borrowed from history.
- **Genre Tree:** Parent Genre → Sub-Genre → Micro-Genre

### 9.3 Tribe Alignment (Vibe Voting System — VVS)

- **Subculture ID:** Juggalo, Goth, Raver, Audiophile, Metalhead
- **Authenticity Score:** "True" vs. "Poser" rating (GVS weighted)
- **Viral Velocity:** Trend status and meme potential.
